Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do movers do?

    Movers are professionals that help you move all your furniture and possessions from one place to another. It could be a residential move or a commercial move. They take precautions not to damage anything in the process, and will place your belongings where you want them in the new location.

  • How much do movers cost in Toronto?

    This depends on how much there is to move and how far the move is. The time, number of movers required for the job, and number of trucks needed all play a factor in determining the cost.

  • How much do you tip movers?

    This depends on how satisfied you are with their work. A move is hard work, so movers appreciate their efforts being acknowledged and are grateful for any tips. A good rule of thumb for tipping is 15-20 percent of the total moving bill.

  • Do I need to be present during pickup or delivery?

    Typically, yes. It is customary to be present for pickup and delivery. However, sometimes due to extenuating circumstances, this cannot always happen, in which case another plan of action can be put in place.

  • How can I make moving easier?

    You can have everything ready to go, as well as measure the dimensions of your doorways to be sure that everything will fit. Doing things like making sure electronics are unplugged, the cords are wrapped, and boxes properly packed will help speed up the process. You can also offer refreshments (this is always appreciated). Also, make sure that young children or pets do not get in the way-the last thing a mover would want is to have an accident while carrying something heavy that could harm anyone.

  • Do movers pack clothes?

    Movers will provide boxes and pack clothes, but it is up to you how comfortable you are with them packing certain clothing items.

  • How long does it take for movers to complete a move?

    This depends on the distance they have to travel, how much packing they have to do, and how many items need to be moved.
